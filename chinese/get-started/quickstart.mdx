This quickstart guide will walk you through the process of creating and publishing a custom product to your Shopify store in just a few minutes.

By the end of this guide, you will have a shop that can meet customers' customization needs.

<Tip>**Prerequisites** : Before you begin, make sure to [install the Shopify App]() and [enable the App on your Theme]().</Tip>

## 创建一个定制产品
xxxxxxxx
1. 创建定制产品的方式有两种，一种是导入履行平台提供的产品，另一种是创建自履行的产品，本快速教程以导入履行平台提供的产品为例，如果像了解更多创建定制产品的内容可以访问xxxx
2. 点击“Import Product”按钮，进入创建产品页面
3. 选择一个你想要使用的履行平台，例如：Printify
4. 选择一个你想要销售的产品，例如：T-shirt
5. 选择一个产品的供应商，也可以使用默认第一个供应商，然后点击“Import” 按钮，导入产品

## 创建产品的 mockup
xxxxxxxx

### 创建第一个 mockup
In addition to uploading your own product photos, you can also use images provided by the fulfillment platform.

xxxxxxxxxxx

Click **New Mockup** to open the Mockup Creator and build a mockup for your product.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-40-59.png" />
</Frame>

To create a mockup, please upload a product image first.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-39-59.png" />
</Frame>

Select or upload a front-facing image of the product.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-41-55.png" />
</Frame>

Click the **Using this imag** button.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-43-41.png" />
</Frame>

Click on the **Print Areas** section in the right-hand panel.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-44-53.png" />
</Frame>

Click the **Add Print-Area**.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-28_17-46-08.png" />
</Frame>

1. Select the appropriate front-facing Print Area based on its name, and make note of its aspect ratio.
2. Click **OK** to add this Print Area to your mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_09-55-11.png" />
</Frame>

Drag each of the 4 corners of the Print Area to the appropriate position on the product image.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_09-56-58.png" />
</Frame>

In the Print Areas section of the right-hand panel, change **Mesh Warp** from **2D** to **3D**.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-07-48.png" />
</Frame>

In "3D Mesh Warp" mode, the Print Area’s 4 corner points will be replaced with a 4x4 grid, giving you 16 adjustable control points.
<Frame >
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-18-09.png" />
</Frame>

Using the Print Area’s aspect ratio as a guide, adjust the control points to reshape the Print Area. This helps create a more realistic and natural-looking product mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-19-37.png" />
</Frame>

If your product is white, we recommend changing the **Blending Mode** from **Normal** to **Multiply** in the Print Areas section on the right-hand panel. This will help enhance the mockup’s visual appearance for a more realistic effect.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-20-18.png" />
</Frame>

Click the **Preview** button to open the Personalizer and preview the current mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-21-03.png" />
</Frame>

Test your product in the Personalizer:
1. In the Print Areas panel on the right, select the print area labeled `front_dtg` to start designing.
2. Use the Elements panel on the left to add a design element (such as an image or text) to the canvas.
3. Adjust your design on the canvas and review the product preview in the bottom-left corner to see if it looks the way you want.
4. Click **Back** to return to the Mockup Creator.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-25-26.png" />
</Frame>

If you’re happy with how your mockup looks, click the **Save** button to save your mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-26-16.png" />
</Frame>

</Frame>

Click the **Add Print-Area**
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-34-40.png" />
</Frame>

1. Select the appropriate back-facing Print Area based on its name, and take note of its aspect ratio.
2. Click **OK** to add this Print Area to your mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-37-28.png" />
</Frame>

Using the same steps as before, create a mockup that showcases the back of the product.

  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-43-40.png" />
</Frame>

Test your product in the Personalizer.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-43-52.png" />
</Frame>

If you’re happy with how your mockup looks, click the **Save** button to save your mockup.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-44-22.png" />
</Frame>

## 发布你的定制产品
You can edit settings such as the product URL handle, store currency, and weight unit.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-47-49.png" />
</Frame>

1. In the variants table, select all variants by checking the boxes.
2. Hover over the **Bulk edit** button and select **Bulk Edit Price for Variants** from the dropdown menu.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-52-06.png" />
</Frame>

Easily update prices for all variants in bulk.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_10-58-44.png" />
</Frame>

Once you've set the prices for all variants, click **Done & Publish** to list the product on your Shopify store.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_11-01-07.png" />
</Frame>

Click the **Check on Shopify** button to view the published product in your Shopify store.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/get-started/PixPin_2025-04-29_11-03-27.png" />
</Frame>
